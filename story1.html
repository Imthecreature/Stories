Harry Potter

The Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling is about Harry Potter, an orphan who discovers he is a wizard on his 11th birthday and is accepted into Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The series follows Harry as he navigates the wizarding world, befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and learns about his connection to the evil wizard Lord Voldemort. The series' main conflict is Harry's battle with Voldemort, who wants to become immortal, overthrow the Ministry of Magic, and control all wizards and Muggles. The books' overarching theme is the battle between good and evil, which is personified by Harry and Voldemort.
